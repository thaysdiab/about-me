  • 1,5 kg of Potato
  • 1 liter of oil
  • salt to taste

Preparation Mode:

  1. Cut the smears into toothpicks and dry them with paper towels.

Ingredients:

  • 100 g of snail-type pasta
  • ½ cup (tea) of fresh cream
  • 2 tablespoons of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 pinch of nutmeg powder
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation Mode:

  1. In a bowl mix the pasta, fresh cream, nutmeg, salt and grated Parmesan cheese.
  2. Place an untadover video refractory with butter and take it to the preheated oven at 180ºC for 25 to 30 minutes, or until it is completely browned without becoming dry inside. Serve immediately.
  3. Note: You can add pieces of fried bacon to this mixture.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 tablespoons of powdered chocolate
  • 2 cups of granulated chocolate

Preparation Mode:

  1. Add these ingredients in a pan. Add the chocolate and the butter, and stir until it creates consistency, that is, until it begins to thicken.
  2. Pour everything into a glass bowl and take the refrigerator. Wait approximately an hour and a half, and you're ready, just enjoy it!
