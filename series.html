Favorite Tv Series

Starring Ellen Pompeo (Meredith Gray), Grey's Anatomy is an American medical drama created by Shonda Rhimes.

A kind of hybrid between "The Twilight Zone" and "Tales of the Unexpected", Black Mirror explores feelings of contemporary malaise. Each episode tells a different story, tracing an anthology that shows the dark side of life tied to technology.

La Casa de Papel shows a group of strangers who get together and organize an assault on the Spanish Mint. The third season showed members enjoying the fruits of the theft until the arrest of one of the assailants forces the thieves to come together again to rob the Central Bank of Spain.

Vis a Vis is a Spanish series, which has its exhibition made by Netflix. The series tells the story of Macarena Ferreiro, who needs to get used to his new lifestyle inside a women's prison.

American Horror Story is a horror and suspense series, created by Ryan Murphy and Brad Falchuk. Each season tells a new story centered on a theme and with virtually the entire cast from the previous season playing new and completely different characters.
